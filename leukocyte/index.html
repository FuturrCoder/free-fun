Leukocyte

Version 0.2.1 Alpha (Instructions Below)

============================ Instructions ============================
This is a tower-defense style game about the immune system. Click the bone to earn Glucose and use it to buy White Blood Cells (hover your mouse over the White Blood Cell icon). When the viruses (green) spawn, click the White Blood Cells you bought twice to make them attack a virus. When the White Blood Cells are red, it means they are reloading so you can not use them. The goal of the game is to defend the body (silhouette) and prevent your health from getting to zero. You complete a level by wiping out the wave of infections.

=============================== Items ===============================
White Blood Cell: Description: Moves to a virus and destroys it in 5 seconds.
Speed: 50 px/s. Reload: 15s.
